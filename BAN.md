# PSA: Shard AFK Policy Update
 
> **From Nathan & Pedro — Donut SMP Dev Team**
 
Using bots or alt accounts to AFK shards on accounts that are **not your main account** or an account you **actively play on** is **NOT ALLOWED**.
 
This has been tolerated for a while, but abuse has gotten out of hand — people are gaining large numbers of spawners and items that are then **traded for real money** or used to **flood the economy**.
 
## What You Need to Do
 
**Stop AFKing any accounts that are NOT your main account** or you risk being banned in upcoming ban waves.
 
## Upcoming Changes
 
The dev team will also be making changes to make it harder to AFK if you appear to be a bot, to make the AFKing system **fairer for the overall playerbase**.
